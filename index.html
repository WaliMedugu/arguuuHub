What is Arguuu

Arguments are
underrated.

Most platforms punish disagreement. Arguuu was built for it. Every debate has two sides — and both deserve space to speak, build their case, and let the community decide.

No rage-bait. No clout chasing. Just structured, evidence-grounded debate on the topics that actually matter.

For

Universal basic income reduces poverty and gives people the freedom to pursue meaningful work without the fear of destitution.

Against

Cash transfers without conditions discourage labour participation and do not address the structural causes of poverty.

How it works

Three steps to a
better argument.

01

Pick Your Motion

Browse curated debate topics or propose your own. From politics to philosophy, tech to culture — if it matters, it belongs here.

02

Choose Your Side

Declare FOR or AGAINST. Build your argument point by point, respond to the opposing side, and back your claims with evidence.

03

Let the Room Decide

The community votes — not on who they agree with, but on who argued better. Skill beats echo chambers, every time.

"The point isn't to win. The point is to make the argument worth having."

— The Arguuu Manifesto

Features

Built for substance,
not performance.

Structured Debate Format

Every motion follows a clear structure — opening arguments, rebuttals, closing statements. No shapeless comment threads. Real debate architecture.

Evidence Tagging

Cite sources, tag evidence types, and let the community grade the quality of your proof. Arguments that rely on facts earn more weight.

Skill-Based Voting

Voters don't pick sides — they evaluate arguments. Better logic, stronger evidence, and clearer reasoning move the needle, not popularity.

Reputation System

Build your debater profile across topics and disciplines. Your track record speaks. Respected voices earn the room — not the algorithm.

Debate Rooms

Live-format debates with real-time argument feeds, timed rounds, and audience participation. The arena, not the comment section.

Argument Analytics

See how your argument performed at every stage. Which point landed, what didn't, and where you lost the room. Improve with every debate.

Questions

Common Queries.

How can I start a structured debate?

You can join any active motion on the Arguuu app or propose your own topic. Our structured format ensures every side is heard fairly.

What makes Arguuu different from social media?

Unlike traditional social media, Arguuu enforces a formal debate structure (Opening, Rebuttal, Closing) and uses skill-based voting instead of popularity.

Can I read archived debates online?

Yes, our high-fidelity archive features the most intense and intellectual clashes from the platform, fully accessible via our web hub.
