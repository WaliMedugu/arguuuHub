Common Questions

Getting Started
What is Arguuu?
Arguuu is a structured debate platform designed to foster real, evidence-based arguments. Unlike social media comment sections, Arguuu provides a dedicated space where both sides of a motion (FOR and AGAINST) have equal footing to build their case.
How do I join a debate?
You can browse the live arena or current motions on the home feed. Once you find a topic that interests you, tap into it and choose your side. You can participate by adding your own arguments or voting on existing ones.
Is the app available on iOS and Android?
Currently, we are in beta. Android users can download the APK directly from our landing page. A web version is also available. iOS and official app store releases are coming soon.
The WIT System
What is the WIT Score?
WIT stands for "Win-Intelligence-Track." It's our proprietary metric that reflects your performance in the arena. Your WIT score grows based on your participation, the quality of your arguments as voted by the community, and your debate win record.
How does voting work?
Arguuu uses skill-based voting. Instead of simply agreeing with a side, users are asked to vote on who made the better argument. The weight of your vote may increase as your own WIT score grows, ensuring that the most experienced debaters have a significant voice in judging the room.
Account & Security
Can I change my username?
Yes, you can update your username in your profile settings. Note that your WIT score and history remain tied to your account even if you change your identity.
How do I delete my account?
We respect your right to data privacy. You can request account deletion directly within the app settings or by contacting us at blueparticlestudios@gmail.com. Once requested, all your personal data will be purged within 30 days.
